Jacaranda Estate and Whispering Willows
Cottages near Shimla at Naldehra
 
 
Activities

  • Tattapani is an hour’s drive from Naldehra where white water rafting takes place on the Satluj.
  • Golf bags and balls are available on hire at the golf Club or from us.
  • Horse rides can be arranged.
  • Nature walks; short hikes in the vicinity can be organized.
  • Indoor games are provided.
  • Television with optic fiber based cable connection is provided. DVD’s can be provided with movies on hire at a nominal cost in the room.

 
 
Golf
Tee off in the century old golf course designed by Lord Curzon, gaurded  by tall deodars, and discover the hidden champion in you. (2044m)


Hot Springs and Water Rafting at Tattapani

Visit the hot thermal natural springs an hour away from Naldehra at Tattapani.

Hiking and Trekking
Hiking and treks on Chir pine and Daisy trails to visit ancient temples and villages.


Shimla
Visit Shimla the erstwhile capital of British India and experience the nostalgic atmosphere of the days gone by. The Viceregal Lodge, the library, the church… all well preserved and lit up at night.


Kufri and Fagu
The snow leopard,  and views of snow clad mountains can be savoured in Kufri and Fagu.
